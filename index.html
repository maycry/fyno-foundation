Rebuild Our Ukraine

We created Fyno Foundation, a private nonprofit organization with a mission to help rebuild Ukraine after the war. The existence of this fund is the evidence that we are confident that Ukraine will win, the war will be over and Ukraininas will return to their land.

Our dream

We dream about a bright and safe future for Ukraine and Ukrainians and have already started to take action. Currently we have a small but mighty team with high spirit and energy. We will be covering many causes, including but not limited to these three main pillars:

Renovation and restoration

6.5 millions of Ukrainians were displaced due to the Russian invasion. Thousands of houses were destroyed.

Since the war is not yet over, we plan to provide financial support for efficient and comfortable short term (temporary) living for those who lost their homes.

Going forward we will support sustainable and eco-friendly permanent living options; partial and full restoration of schools and other educational establishments damaged during the invasion: kindergartens, schools, universities and many more.

Education

Ukrainians have always been educated and intelligent nation, we will not let the consequences on the Russian invasion change that.

We plan to provide financial support and create comfortable conditions for students to continue education after returning to Ukraine; sponsor and organize events to spread the awareness about Ukrainian history, language and culture and the consequences of the Russian genocide against Ukrainian nation.

Mental Health

We know that every Ukrainian needs mental health support and psychological help now and will require it for a long time after the war is over.

We will support programs towards healing and treating psychological traumas caused by war for multiple groups including but not limited to: children, women and war veterans.
